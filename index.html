PONG GAME





Instructions for playing the game :-



  1. You will be playing as the left player.
  2. Your opponent(computer-controlled) will be the right player.
  3. You have to move the paddle accordingly on the left side of the black screen using a mouse/cursor in order to not miss the ball.
  4. Missing the ball once would mean that your opponent will gain a score and vice versa.
  5. The game will end once one of the players has gained 5 scores.
  6. You can continue playing the game by clicking on the "PLAY AGAIN" option on the screen.

Good luck 😄✌
